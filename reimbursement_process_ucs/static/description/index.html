On debugger mode and go to settings and in that open group and search for Reimbursement group , the user in this group has access of only for request for reimbursement and admin has approved access.

now got to Reimbursements app Click the "New" button (located on the left, near "Reimbursements") to start a fresh reimbursement application.

Fill in all the required details for your reimbursement, such as the reimbursement type, description, and amount. Attach any necessary proof or documents. Once all details are entered, click the "Submit" button. After submission, the request will move to a "Submitted" state, awaiting approval.

An approver will review the submitted reimbursement request. Click the "Approve" button (located on the left, next to "Reject"). Once approved, the status of the reimbursement request will change to "Approved."

This step is typically performed by an administrator to set up user access. Navigate to "Users" settings (usually under "Settings" or "Administration"). Under the "Reimbursement" section, ensure that the appropriate checkbox ("Admin" or "User") is selected for each user to grant them the necessary permissions for the reimbursement module.
